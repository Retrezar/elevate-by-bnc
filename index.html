Programme Curriculum

6 Core Modules.
Built for Practitioners.

Not legal theory. A practitioner-led session — understand the DPDP Act, map your exposure, and start your compliance framework before you leave the room.

01
DPDP Foundations
Key definitions, scope of the Act, applicability to your business, and Data Protection Board powers.
02
Consent Framework
Valid consent, consent notices, deemed consent — WhatsApp, mailing lists, and CRMs are all in scope.
03
Data Principal Rights
Access, correction, erasure rights. Your business must respond within 72 hours — or face penalties.
04
Breach Notification
Definition of a breach, mandatory reporting timelines to the Data Protection Board, and response planning.
05
Third-Party & Cross-Border
Vendor obligations, data transfer risk, cross-border data processing requirements under DPDPA.
06
90-Day Compliance Roadmap
Conduct a readiness audit, prioritise actions, and walk out with a personalised 90-day compliance plan.
